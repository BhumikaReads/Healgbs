⚡ Chapter 1 · What Happened

Heer's immune system
got confused.

Heer has something called Guillain-Barré Syndrome — GBS. Big name, but here's what it actually means:

🦠 A few weeks ago Heer's body fought a small infection — maybe a cold she barely noticed. Her immune system won that fight. But then it got confused and didn't stop fighting. Instead it accidentally attacked her own nerve coating.

Think of Heer's nerves like electrical wires running from her brain to every part of her body. Wrapped around those wires is a coating called myelin — like plastic insulation on a cable. GBS stripped some of that away.

🔬 Healthy nerve — signal travels fast & clear:
Myelin (insulation) Signal Nerve fibre
⚠️ GBS nerve — myelin stripped, signal barely gets through:

Result: signals can't reach muscles → lost speech, swallowing, face movement, hand strength

💚 The wires are still there. The myelin can grow back — and it is already growing back inside Heer right now.
🧬 Chapter 2 · Heer's Specific Case

Heer's GBS had a
special fingerprint.

Most GBS attacks start from the legs upward. Heer's hit her face, eyes, speech, and swallowing hardest. The doctors found specific antibodies in her blood that explain exactly why.

🎯 Normally antibodies are secret weapons — tiny proteins that lock onto germs and destroy them. But some of Heer's were mistakenly made to lock onto her own nerve parts — especially the ones controlling her face, eyes, and throat.

Finding those antibodies is actually good news — it means the doctors know exactly what's causing the problem. Like finally finding the leaking pipe instead of just mopping the floor.

💪 Chapter 3 · What Heer Has Already Survived

Heer already fought
two massive battles.

Heer has had 11 treatment sessions — 6 plasma exchanges + 5 IVIG. That is enormous. Here's what each one actually did:

🩸
Plasma Exchange — 6 Sessions
They took Heer's blood out, filtered it through a machine that removed the harmful antibodies, and put clean blood back in. Like draining a contaminated pool and refilling with fresh water — 6 times.
💉
IVIG — 5 Sessions

⚠️ Both treatments cleaned the mess and slowed the attack — but they couldn't turn off the factory still making bad antibodies. That's what the next treatment does.
